# Stir-fried celery with dried aroma

![芹菜炒香干](/images/芹菜炒香干.jpg)

## Ingredients

- celery
- Dried fragrant
- Red pepper
- Soybean oil
- Salt
- Chicken Essence

## step

- Add 120g soybean oil, 1100g celery, 500g dried fragrant, 60g red pepper, 15g salt, 10g chicken essence
- Produced in 3 minutes with hot stir-fry.
