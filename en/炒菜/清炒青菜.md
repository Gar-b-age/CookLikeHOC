# Stir-fried green vegetables

![清炒青菜](/images/清炒青菜.jpg)

## raw material

- Green vegetables
- Soybean oil
- Cooked lard
- Salt
- Chicken Essence
- White sugar

## step

- Add 100g soybean oil, 40g cooked lard, 1300g greens, 12g salt, 6g chicken essence, 3g white sugar
- Stir-fry in hot 2 minutes.
