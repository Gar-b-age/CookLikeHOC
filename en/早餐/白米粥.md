# White rice porridge

![白米粥](/images/白米粥.png)

## Ingredients

- Porridge
- Rice
- Sticky rice

## step

- Clean 320g of porridge, 80g of rice, 100g of glutinous rice and put 8500g of water in
In the stainless steel drum, boil it over high heat and boil it for 60 minutes before it can be produced.
