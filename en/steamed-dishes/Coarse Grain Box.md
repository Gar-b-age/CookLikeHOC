# Coarse Grain Box

![Coarse Grain Box](../../images/%E7%B2%97%E7%B2%AE%E7%9B%92.png)

## Ingredients

- Corn chunks
- Pumpkin chunks
- Sweet potato chunks
- Red dates

## Steps

- 1. Place 90g pumpkin (two pieces), 100g sweet potato (two pieces), and 100g corn (two pieces) into a steamer and steam for 25 minutes
